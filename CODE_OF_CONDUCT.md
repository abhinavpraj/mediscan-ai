# Code of Conduct

MediScan AI follows the Contributor Covenant principles.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and inclusive.
- Assume good intent while addressing harmful behavior directly.
- Keep technical discussion focused on evidence and user impact.
- Do not share private patient data in issues, commits, or screenshots.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior harms the community or compromises privacy.
